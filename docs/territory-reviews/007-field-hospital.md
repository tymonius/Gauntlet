# 007. Field Hospital

**Status:** Approved  
**Source:** `releases/v0.5.7/Gauntlet_v0.5.7_Canonical_Data.json`

## v0.5.7 source

> After a battle on this Territory, its controller may place one card they played that would enter their Graveyard in their discard pile instead.

## v0.6 decision

- **Complexity:** Basic
- **Watchlist:** Graveyard denial and repeated recovery during prolonged fighting

> During battle cleanup after a battle on Field Hospital, its controller may place one card they played that would go to their Graveyard in their discard pile instead.

## Rationale

- Keep the mechanic unchanged.
- Clarify that the effect is a replacement during battle cleanup.
- The effect does not retrieve a card that has already entered the Graveyard.
- The effect does not interfere with cards moved to the Graveyard immediately during the battle.
