# 014. Old Battlefield

**Status:** Approved for retention pending playtest  
**Source:** `releases/v0.5.7/Gauntlet_v0.5.7_Canonical_Data.json`

## v0.5.7 source

> After a battle on this Territory, its controller may place one unplayed card from their battle draw in their Graveyard instead of their discard pile.

## v0.6 decision

- **Complexity:** Basic
- **Watchlist:** Narrow utility, Arcane Graveyard setup, and deliberate deck thinning

> During battle cleanup after a battle on Old Battlefield, its controller may place one unplayed card from their battle draw in their Graveyard instead of their discard pile.

## Rationale

- Keep the mechanic for now while flagging it for usefulness testing.
- The primary purpose is controlled Graveyard setup for cards and faction effects that benefit from a chosen card being there.
- It can also permanently remove a weak or situational card from future reshuffles because the Graveyard is never reshuffled.
- Outside Graveyard-focused or deliberate deck-thinning strategies, the effect may be neutral or harmful, so its practical value should be tested rather than assumed.
- Clarify that this is a destination choice during battle cleanup, not movement from the discard pile after cleanup.
