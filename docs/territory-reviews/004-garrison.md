# 004. Garrison

**Status:** Approved  
**Source:** `releases/v0.5.7/Gauntlet_v0.5.7_Canonical_Data.json`

## v0.5.7 source

> When defending this Territory, its controller draws four battle cards instead of three.

## v0.6 decision

- **Complexity:** Basic
- **Watchlist:** Effects that increase or reduce the initial battle draw

> When Garrison's controller defends it, they draw one additional card for their initial battle draw.

## Rationale

- Keep the mechanic while making it additive instead of replacing the battle-draw size.
- This allows Garrison to stack cleanly with other effects that modify the initial battle draw.
- The Territory improves card selection without increasing the number of battle-drawn cards the defender may play.
