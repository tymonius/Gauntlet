## Integrated cross-card rules

The following approved amendments are included in `../Gauntlet_v0.6_Working_Rules.md`:

- Conditions do not exist in v0.6.
- Whenever a player could play an Action card during their turn, they may instead discard one banked Asset they control. This uses that Action opportunity and is not an Action-card play. An additional Action-card play may be exchanged for voluntary Asset removal unless a card says otherwise.
- A negated card has no effect and follows its normal destination unless an effect says otherwise.
- General Territory activation, suppression, Overlay replacement, dormant lower Overlays, and Ruins rules are consolidated.
- Refused Terms distinguish a Diplomat win, a Diplomat loss, and a battle ending without a winner. Only an actual loss forfeits staked Influence.
- The Inquisition Arcane-trait trigger is named **Blasphemy**.
- The 1-Conviction Purge retains the top-discard option and adds an alternative that condemns up to two discard cards with combined value 2 or less.
- Effects that resolve another Battle effect cannot recurse indefinitely; **Heresy** explicitly permits one additional copied-effect layer before the chain must stop.

- The v0.5.7 card **Arcane Knowledge** is named **Witchcraft** in v0.6 and retains its approved cost-5 Arcane-faction mechanics.
- The v0.5.7 card **Witchcraft** is named **Arcane Knowledge** in v0.6, becomes a cost-4 Neutral card with the Arcane trait, and is recorded in `054-arcane-knowledge.md`.
- Arcane trait and Arcane allegiance are separate.
- The former **The Black Edict** Neutral reset is named **Sequestration**; **The Black Edict** is reserved for a future Inquisition card.
- **Manifest Destiny** is finalized as the Unique Neutral cost-5 Territory-expansion capstone.
- Eleven existing Neutral cards received approved cost reductions during the cost-curve audit.
- Twelve new Neutral cards complete the 50-card pool.
- **Counterworks** gives Neutral narrow Overlay prevention and temporary suppression; Repair and deep Overlay management remain reserved for the future Engineer faction.
- The Military package is complete at 12 cards with a 1 / 4 / 3 / 3 / 1 curve, 35 total unique-card value, and 2.92 average cost.
- **Shock and Awe** is the Unique cost-5 Military statement card.
- **War Crimes** is now Military rather than Inquisition.
- **Militias** and **Patriotism** are retired from the v0.6 playable pool.
- **Standing Orders** is deferred and not included in the Military release package.
- The Diplomat package is complete at 12 cards with a 1 / 3 / 5 / 2 / 1 curve, 35 total unique-card value, and 2.92 average cost.

- The Inquisition package is complete at 12 cards with a 1 / 3 / 4 / 2 / 2 curve, 37 total unique-card value, and 3.08 average cost.
