## Approved rule amendments awaiting rollup

- Whenever a player could play an Action card during their turn, they may instead discard one banked Asset they control. This uses that Action opportunity and is not an Action-card play. An additional Action-card play may be exchanged for voluntary Asset removal unless a card says otherwise.
- A card effect that resolves another card's Battle effect cannot select a Battle effect that would itself resolve another card's Battle effect.

## Approved cross-card changes awaiting rollup

- The v0.5.7 card **Arcane Knowledge** is renamed **Witchcraft** in v0.6 and retains its previously approved cost-5 Arcane-faction mechanics.
- The v0.5.7 card **Witchcraft** is renamed **Arcane Knowledge** in v0.6, becomes a cost-4 Neutral card with the Arcane trait, and is recorded in `054-arcane-knowledge.md`.
- Arcane trait and Arcane allegiance are separate: Arcane-trait cards may appear in the Neutral pool, while the Arcane faction retains the greatest density and strongest systemic exploitation of them.
