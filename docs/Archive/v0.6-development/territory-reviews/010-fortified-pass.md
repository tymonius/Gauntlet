# 010. Fortified Pass

**Status:** Approved  
**Source:** `releases/v0.5.7/Gauntlet_v0.5.7_Canonical_Data.json`

## v0.5.7 source

> When this Territory's controller defends it, the attacker's Assets are inactive for that battle.

## v0.6 decision

- **Complexity:** Basic
- **Watchlist:** Asset-heavy faction matchups and suppression stacking

> When Fortified Pass's controller defends it, the attacker's banked Assets are inactive during that battle.

## Rationale

- Keep the mechanic unchanged.
- Clarify that the effect applies to banked Assets.
- Preserve the controller requirement, so the effect does not benefit an opponent occupying the Territory or its controller while counterattacking to reclaim it.
