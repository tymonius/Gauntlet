# 020. Toll Bridge

**Status:** Approved  
**Source:** `releases/v0.5.7/Gauntlet_v0.5.7_Canonical_Data.json`

## v0.5.7 source

> To advance from this Territory, a player must discard one card from their hand.

## v0.6 decision

- **Complexity:** Basic
- **Watchlist:** Hand denial and movement-bonus interactions

> To voluntarily advance from Toll Bridge, a player must discard one card from their hand. If they cannot, they cannot advance from it.

## Rationale

- Preserve the discard toll for forward movement.
- Clarify that the cost applies only to voluntary advance.
- Withdrawal, retreat, and forced movement are unaffected.
- A player with no card in hand cannot voluntarily advance from Toll Bridge.
