# 016. Command Tent

**Status:** Approved  
**Source:** `releases/v0.5.7/Gauntlet_v0.5.7_Canonical_Data.json`

## v0.5.7 source

> If you start your turn occupying and controlling this Territory, you may play one Action card before movement and one Action card after movement. If Command Tent is face down, you may reveal it at the start of your turn without using either Action opportunity.

## v0.6 decision

- **Complexity:** Advanced
- **Watchlist:** Action combinations and effects that grant additional Action plays

> If a player begins their turn occupying and controlling Command Tent, they may play one Action card before movement and one Action card after movement that turn. If Command Tent is face down, they may reveal it at the beginning of that turn without using either Action play.

## Rationale

- Keep the mechanic unchanged while using third-person wording consistent with other Territories.
- Establish the permission at the beginning of the turn, so the player retains the post-movement Action play even if they move away from Command Tent.
- Allow a face-down Command Tent to reveal itself in time to provide its benefit without consuming either Action play.
- Command Tent modifies only the normal Action-card limit. Each player's normal Battle-card commitment from hand remains separate and unaffected.
