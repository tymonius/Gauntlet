# 005. Disrupted Supply Lines

**Status:** Approved  
**Source:** `releases/v0.5.7/Gauntlet_v0.5.7_Canonical_Data.json`

## v0.5.7 source

> While a player is on this Territory, they may have no more than one face-up Asset. Turn their other Assets face down and inactive until they leave.

## v0.6 decision

- **Complexity:** Advanced
- **Watchlist:** Asset-heavy factions and active/inactive timing

> While a player occupies Disrupted Supply Lines, only one banked Asset they control can be active. They choose which one remains active; all their other banked Assets are inactive until they leave.

## Rationale

- Preserve the mechanic while removing reliance on face-up and face-down Asset status.
- Suppress an Asset engine without destroying the player's setup or changing their Asset-bank capacity.
- Let the affected player choose which Asset remains active.
- Restore all suppressed Assets automatically when the player leaves.
