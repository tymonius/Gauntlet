# 003. Difficult Terrain

**Status:** Approved  
**Source:** `releases/v0.5.7/Gauntlet_v0.5.7_Canonical_Data.json`

## v0.5.7 source

> When a player enters this Territory, their movement ends for the turn. A player who starts their turn here or enters it during their turn cannot play an Action card after movement that turn.

## v0.6 decision

- **Complexity:** Basic
- **Watchlist:** Extra-movement and extra-Action interactions

> When a player enters Difficult Terrain, their movement ends for the turn. A player who begins their turn there or enters it during their turn cannot play an Action card after movement that turn.

## Rationale

- Keep the mechanic essentially unchanged.
- Name Difficult Terrain directly for clarity outside the physical card.
- Preserve the player's ability to use an Action before movement.
- Ending movement for the turn also prevents later bonus movement from carrying the player farther after entry.
