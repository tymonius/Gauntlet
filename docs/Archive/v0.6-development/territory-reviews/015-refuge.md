# 015. Refuge

**Status:** Approved  
**Source:** `releases/v0.5.7/Gauntlet_v0.5.7_Canonical_Data.json`

## v0.5.7 source

> When a player voluntarily withdraws onto this Territory, they draw one card.

## v0.6 decision

- **Complexity:** Basic
- **Watchlist:** Repeatable withdrawal effects and card-draw loops

> After a player voluntarily withdraws onto Refuge, they draw one card.

## Rationale

- Keep the mechanic unchanged.
- Clarify that the withdrawal must actually place the player on Refuge before the draw occurs.
- The effect does not trigger from retreat after losing a battle, forced movement, or normal movement onto Refuge.
