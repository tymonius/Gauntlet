# 018. Training Grounds

**Status:** Approved  
**Source:** `releases/v0.5.7/Gauntlet_v0.5.7_Canonical_Data.json`

## v0.5.7 source

> When defending this Territory, its controller may discard their entire battle draw and draw the same number of replacement cards. They must use the replacement draw.

## v0.6 decision

- **Complexity:** Advanced
- **Watchlist:** Increased battle-draw amounts and low-deck reshuffling

> When Training Grounds's controller defends it, after drawing their initial battle draw but before choosing cards to play from it, they may discard that entire draw and draw the same number of replacement cards. If they do, the replacement cards become their battle draw.

## Rationale

- Preserve the full battle-draw mulligan.
- Clarify that the decision occurs after the initial draw but before choosing any battle-drawn cards to play.
- Replace the original draw completely; the player cannot return to cards from the discarded draw.
- Clarify that using the replacement draw does not require the player to play a card from it.
- Scale the replacement amount with any effect that modifies the initial battle draw.
