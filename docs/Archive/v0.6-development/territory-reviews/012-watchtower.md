# 012. Watchtower

**Status:** Approved  
**Source:** `releases/v0.5.7/Gauntlet_v0.5.7_Canonical_Data.json`

## v0.5.7 source

> When the defender controls this Territory, any card the attacker commits from hand during the battle commitment step is placed face up instead of face down. Cards played from the battle draw are not hand commitments.

## v0.6 decision

- **Complexity:** Basic
- **Watchlist:** Commitment timing and information-effect interactions

> When Watchtower's controller defends it, the attacker commits any Battle card from hand face up instead of face down.

## Rationale

- Keep the mechanic unchanged.
- The effect applies only to the attacker's Battle-card commitment from hand.
- Battle-drawn cards follow their normal process and remain unaffected.
- The committed card becomes public immediately without changing its resolution or normal destination.
