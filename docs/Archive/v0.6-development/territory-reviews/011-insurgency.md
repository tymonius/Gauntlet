# 011. Insurgency

**Status:** Approved  
**Source:** `releases/v0.5.7/Gauntlet_v0.5.7_Canonical_Data.json`

## v0.5.7 source

> While an opponent occupies this Territory without controlling it, their Assets are inactive.

## v0.6 decision

- **Complexity:** Basic
- **Watchlist:** Asset-heavy faction matchups and occupation-window suppression

> While an opponent occupies Insurgency without controlling it, their banked Assets are inactive.

## Rationale

- Keep the mechanic unchanged.
- Clarify that the effect applies to banked Assets.
- Suppress an invader's Assets only during the occupation window before capture.
- End the suppression when the occupying opponent leaves or takes control of the Territory.
