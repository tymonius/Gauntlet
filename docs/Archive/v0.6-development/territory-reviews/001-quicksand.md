# 001. Quicksand

**Status:** Approved  
**Source:** `releases/v0.5.7/Gauntlet_v0.5.7_Canonical_Data.json`

## v0.5.7 source

> If a player begins their Movement phase on this Territory, they cannot voluntarily withdraw or move more than one tile that turn. Forced movement is unaffected.

## v0.6 decision

- **Complexity:** Basic
- **Watchlist:** None

> If a player begins their Movement phase on Quicksand, they cannot voluntarily withdraw or move more than one space that turn. Forced movement is unaffected.

## Rationale

- Keep the mechanic unchanged.
- Replace **tile** with **space** for current movement terminology.
- Name Quicksand directly so the effect remains clear when quoted or referenced outside the physical card.
- Preserve the distinction between voluntary movement and forced movement.
