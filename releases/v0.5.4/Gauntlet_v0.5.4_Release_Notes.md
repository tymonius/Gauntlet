# Gauntlet v0.5.4 - Last Stand Patch

Status: pre-release physical playtest edition  
Date: June 21, 2026

## Rule change

**Last Stand:** When defending in your own Heartland, you have Homeland Advantage and gain +1 to your battle total.

Heartlands are not Territories, cannot be controlled, and cannot receive Territory effects or Overlays unless a card explicitly says otherwise.

## Rules preserved from v0.5.3

- Cards committed from hand during battle go to the Graveyard.
- Cards played from the battle draw normally go to the discard pile.
- Attrition's Action effect redirects the opponent's played battle-draw cards to the Graveyard when that opponent loses.
- Attrition's Battle effect sends all cards from the losing opponent's initial battle draw to the Graveyard.
