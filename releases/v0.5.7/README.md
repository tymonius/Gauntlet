## Printing

Card sheets use exact 2.5 x 3.5 inch poker-card fronts in a 3 x 3 US Letter layout. Print at **Actual Size / 100%**, with scaling disabled. For a durable prototype, cut the fronts and sleeve them in opaque standard poker sleeves with ordinary playing cards or bulk trading cards behind them.

Territory cards are still cut to standard poker-card size, but their text is printed in landscape orientation so the card can face the controlling player on the board.

## Rules changes incorporated

- Heartland cards removed. Heartlands are part of the Gauntlet layout, not cards.
- Heartlands are not Territories and do not count for effects that count Territories or Asset bank limits.
- Deck construction clarified: a deck is at least 30 playable cards with total value no more than 60.
- Supplemental Cards added as a rules category for turn order, battle order, Asset bank, and capture references.
- Homeland Advantage, Heartland defense, tied battles, card drawing/reshuffling, partial draws, battle-draw amount, passing, Conscription, Watchtower, Embargo, and canceled-card destinations clarified from `docs/v0.5.7_rules_clarifications.md`.
- Likely faction-specific cards are removed from default v0.5.7 core play; advanced neutral cards remain available for testing.
