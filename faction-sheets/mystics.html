Gauntlet v0.6 — Mystics Faction Sheets

Three Letter-size 9-up sheets containing the twelve Mystics playable cards, Alchemist, Spirit Walker, the Mystics Reference, three Rite fronts, and three completed Rite backs.

Print at 100% scale with browser headers and footers disabled and background graphics enabled. Cut cards to 2.5 × 3.5 inches. Page 1 is single-sided. Pages 2 and 3 are aligned for long-edge duplex printing: incomplete Rite fronts occupy the bottom row of page 2 and mirrored completed Rite backs occupy the bottom row of page 3.
