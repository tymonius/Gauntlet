Gauntlet v0.6.0 Faction Sheets

Browser-printable cards and supplemental components for all six v0.6.0 factions.

Military faction sheets
Twelve playable cards, General, Commandant, and sliding Command tracker.
Diplomat faction sheets
Twelve playable cards, Ambassador, Senator, nine Proposal / Treaty Article pairs, two reference faces, and a 0–10 sliding Influence Tracker.
Financier faction sheets
Twelve playable cards, Banker, Executive, faction reference, Capital tracker, and eight generic Deed cards.
Intelligence faction sheets
Twelve playable cards, Ranger, Spymaster, two faction references, and the Intelligence resource tracker.
Mystics faction sheets
Twelve playable cards, Alchemist, Spirit Walker, faction reference, and three Rite front/back pairs.
Inquisition faction sheets
Twelve playable cards, Grand Inquisitor, Witch Hunter, two rules references, and a 0–4 Conviction tracker.
