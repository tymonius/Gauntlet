Gauntlet v0.6 — Intelligence Faction Sheets

Two Letter-size 9-up sheets containing the twelve Intelligence playable cards, Ranger, Spymaster, two faction references, one sliding Intel Tracker, and one sliding Operation Progress Tracker.

Place each tracker beneath its paired Reference Card, fully covered at 0, then slide the Reference upward until its bottom edge aligns with the current value. No markers are required.

Print at 100% scale with browser headers and footers disabled and background graphics enabled. Cut to 2.5 × 3.5 inches.
